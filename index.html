What is Body Mass Index:

BMI or Body Mass Index is a parameter used by the World Health Organization to calculate the ideal weight of each person. The index is calculated by dividing the person's weight by the person's height squared.

Want to find out your BMI? Enter your weight and height in the calculator and compare with the indexes in the table.

WARNING: BMI is not the only aspect to be observed to determine obesity. This is because the weight composition can be affected by the person's bone structures, which means that a BMI above the ideal range does not necessarily mean excess fat.

However, the index is quite useful in detecting a weight below or above the recommended one. Thus, it can be used as a personal compass to lead to a healthier life. In any case you should consult a nutritionist!

Always check you health status with a doctor!
